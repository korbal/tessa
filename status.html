The Master Status System

One Subscriber, One Status, Zero Chaos

The Core Concept

Every subscriber in your ConvertKit has exactly ONE status at any given time. This status determines what emails they receive and what happens next.

Think of it like a traffic control system: each subscriber is a car that can only be in one lane at a time. The status tells us which lane they're in.

The Magic: When a subscriber changes status, the system automatically pauses the old sequence and starts the new one. No manual work. No duplicate emails. No chaos.

‚è∞ The One-Year Cleanup Rule

If a subscriber stays in Prospect or Waitlist status for 12 months without buying (meaning they've seen ~5 launch opportunities), they're automatically unsubscribed.

Why? Your audience has a finite lifecycle. Once they land their consultant job, they're gone anyway. This keeps your list healthy and engaged.
